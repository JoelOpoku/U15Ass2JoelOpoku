Our story at Whisker’s Café

Discover the passion and purpose behind Whisker's Café, where our love for cats meets the joy of a perfect brew. We invite you to learn more about our unique space in Oldham, United Kingdom.

Our inspiration: a love for cats and community

We've always been captivated by the nature, care, and companionship of cats. For thousands of years, cats have enriched our lives, and at Whisker's Café, we wanted to create a special place that celebrates this enduring bond. Our main idea was simple: to combine the comfort of a warm drink with the delightful presence of our feline friends.

What makes Whisker’s Café special?

At Whisker's Café, we pride ourselves on offering an experience unlike any other. Our café is home to a delightful family of unique cats, each with their own personality waiting to greet you. Beyond our furry residents, we craft unique drinks that tantalise your taste buds.
